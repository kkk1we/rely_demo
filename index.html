Services

Services that rely platform offers

Smart contract creation

Rely is able to accurately interpret natural use of language, and produce a legally binding contractual agreement between two parties, and the smart contract required for the blockchain functionality of Rely. 

De-centrelized escrow holdings

The deposit by the customer can only be released by the conditions stated in Rely's DPoS smart contracts. This makes the contract tamper proof, and reliable. Once the release conditions are fulfilled, the funds are released immediately to the contractor,  delivering timely payment to contractors

Robust and fair dispute settlement

When there is a dispute and both parties vote against each other in the release of funds, the RELY team will review the case, and use its votes to choose to release or withhold payment to contractor.

Formal Contract Creator
This is the demo area for Rely.

This is a demo area that demonstrates the workflow of using our platform rely. This will take you through a simulation of contract creation and natural language understanding, which makes contract creation easier. once the user inputs their contracts in a natural way, the program will create a more legally worded and formal contract, and a smart Econtract that rely uses. Then, specific milestones are derived from the contract, and release conditions defined. After the milestones are defined, then customers and contractors can vote on whether to release funds to contractors or not.

Convert Layman Terms into Stricter and More Legally Binding Contracts

Customer wants contractor to build a pavement around a well for customer, at X address by Y deadline, ensuring quality using some quantitative metric.

Clearer and More Formal Contract

Clause: The Contractor agrees to construct a pavement for the well located at [X Address], in accordance with the agreed-upon specifications, and to complete the work no later than [Y Deadline]. The construction shall adhere to the following quality standards and metrics: Metric of Quality: The pavement must meet the following criteria: Durability: The pavement shall be constructed using materials that conform to [relevant local building codes or ASTM standards, e.g., ASTM C33 for aggregates]. The surface must withstand a minimum compressive strength of [specific value, e.g., 30 MPa (megapascals)] at 28 days. Surface Evenness: Variations in the surface level shall not exceed [e.g., 5 mm over a 2-meter span] when measured with a straightedge. Drainage: The pavement shall allow for proper drainage, with a slope of [specific percentage, e.g., 1-2%] away from the well to prevent water pooling. Workmanship: All joints and edges must be smooth and free from visible cracks, chips, or irregularities. Longevity: The pavement must maintain its integrity and functionality for a minimum period of [e.g., 5 years] under normal use conditions. Inspection and Approval: Upon completion, the pavement will be inspected by the Customer or a designated third-party inspector to ensure compliance with the above metrics. The Contractor agrees to rectify any deficiencies at no additional cost if the pavement fails to meet these standards.

Customer deposit

To initiate the contractual agreement between two parties, the customer will deposit the sum required to fulfil the contract, and will be given 45% of voting rights for release. Contractor will be given another 45% of voting release conditions. The 10% of the remaining voting will be given to rely team to tip the votes in the case of a dispute

    pay as you receive

    Pay as you receive the contractor's services through milestones, only pay once a certain milestone is reached. 

      In case of dispute...

      In case of dispute, if both parties vote against each other for the release of funds, the Rely team will review the transactions and services received on a case by case basis and determine whether funds should be released or refunded back to the customer.

        if all goes well...

        If all goes well, and the contract is completed, when the last milestone is achieved and approved by both sides, the funds will be released to the contractor. Both the customers and contractors are satisfied as the customers receive the desired service and quality, and contractors receive payment. During the whole process, the customer has peace of mind because the contractor cannot get payment without providing service, and contractors are trusting of customers because as long as service is delivered, the payment will be released.
