Services

Services that rely platform offers

Smart contract creation

Rely is able to accurately interpret natural use of language, and produce a legally binding contractual agreement between two parties, and the smart contract required for the blockchain functionality of Rely. 

De-centrelized escrow holdings

The deposit by the customer can only be released by the conditions stated in Rely's DPoS smart contracts. This makes the contract tamper proof, and reliable. Once the release conditions are fulfilled, the funds are released immediately to the contractor,  delivering timely payment to contractors

Robust and fair dispute settlement

When there is a dispute and both parties vote against each other in the release of funds, the RELY team will review the case, and use its votes to choose to release or withhold payment to contractor.

Customer deposit

To initiate the contractual agreement between two parties, the customer will deposit the sum required to fulfil the contract, and will be given 45% of voting rights for release. Contractor will be given another 45% of voting release conditions. The 10% of the remaining voting will be given to rely team to tip the votes in the case of a dispute

    pay as you receive

    Pay as you receive the contractor's services through milestones, only pay once a certain milestone is reached. 

      In case of dispute...

      In case of dispute, if both parties vote against each other for the release of funds, the Rely team will review the transactions and services received on a case by case basis and determine whether funds should be released or refunded back to the customer.

        if all goes well...

        If all goes well, and the contract is completed, when the last milestone is achieved and approved by both sides, the funds will be released to the contractor. Both the customers and contractors are satisfied as the customers receive the desired service and quality, and contractors receive payment. During the whole process, the customer has peace of mind because the contractor cannot get payment without providing service, and contractors are trusting of customers because as long as service is delivered, the payment will be released.
